Welcome to our Rathore family website of Kasumbi in Rajasthan state of India.
We belong to the royal Rajput clan of western India, the Rathore's.
Rathore dynasty ruled a number of kingdoms and princely states in Rajasthan and
neighboring states of western India before India's independence in 1947.
The largest and oldest among these was Jodhpur, in Marwar and Bikaner.
Rathore dynasty is divided into 40 plus branches and we are the decendents of Rao.
we are also known by the name of great Rao Jodha.
